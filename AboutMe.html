ABOUT

My Journey

After gaining my Masters in Electronic Engineering I moved into education, eventually becoming Head of Data for a large school. This challenged me to find patterns, make predictions and present recommend actions based on my analyses.

My main skills are in understanding and disseminating information. With a creative insight and a curious outlook, I have a knack for uncovering often missed patterns and links within datasets. Able to draw conclusions and make clear recommendations, I give power to people to make direct actions to improve their outcomes in fast moving environments.

Throughout my career in education, I relied heavily on placing data at the forefront of decision making. A passion for explaining complex information through clear conclusions and visualisations led me to further my education in data analytics, including data collection, cleaning, and manipulation. With my skills I have made predictions and present recommend actions based on my analyses, to improve the outcomes for pupils. I have run projects on:

  • Generating bespoke learning books for pupils using comparative analysis of needs by creating statistical models, completed using Excel and Python’s OpenPyXL library in PyCharm, with GMail API integration.
  • Determine the staffing needs of medical facilities to provide best care during influenza season, conducted and presented using Excel and Tableau.
  • Created recommendations for ecommerce market based on customer buying habits using NumPy, Pandas and MatPlotLib in the Jupyter environment.
